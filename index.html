The culturally kaleidoscopic island of Puerto Rico remains the best kept secret in the Caribbean

intro: >

A spellbinding land, Puerto Rico fuses colonial Spanish flavour with American style, with a people known for warm, vivacious hospitality making any stay a treat. Its astounding beauty will captivate you upon arrival, be it the multi-coloured patchwork of colonial buildings knitted together by cobbled streets, or the tropical rainforests that bleed serendipitously into golden shorelines with azure blue waters. It’s a wholesome, inviting scene that is ripe for exploring.

Those with an active disposition won’t be disappointed; Puerto Rico offers some of the best surfing and scuba diving spots around – with unique natural phenomenon, like the breathtaking Laguna Grande, providing the icing on the cake. Or choose to mix with the heady culture of this Caribbean jewel, and explore the culinary delights of the island, the endless supply of quality rum, and the hip-shaking sounds of salsa.

Puerto Rico is a hot, frantic, and simultaneously laid-back den of complete escape, ready and waiting to fulfil your Caribbean wanderlust.
