If you love to get the adrenaline flowing, Puerto Rico is a hotbed for adventure. Zipline over rainforest canopies, rappel down deep, cavernous caves, catch some waves or immerse yourself in the islands’ equestrian heritage. Your adventure starts here.

sections: - title: Surfing in Rincon class: surfing-in-rincon content: >

At the remotest corner of the island, Rincón is a wild, untamed spot where the sunsets shimmer red and the vibe is relaxed. This is surfer’s paradise. With plenty old guard hippies left from the 1968 Surfing World Championships, the area exudes an alternative feel, and offers some of the best waves in the Northern hemisphere. With a choice of beaches and plenty of rental places and schools, you’ll be all set to catch the perfect wave.

- title: Horseback riding class: horseback-riding content: >

Puerto Rico has a long equestrian tradition, particularly famed for its Paso Fino breed. This centuries old tradition continues to this day, with plenty of riding schools offering tours along stunning trails. Enjoy the relaxed ride that the Paso Fino is known for, whilst taking in incredible landscapes.

- title: Scuba diving class: scuba-diving content: >

Venture deep into the life aquatic, every bit as diverse and breathtaking as what’s available on land. From a supremely rich biodiversity, including vast coral reefs and plenty of colourful marine life, to gargantuan underwater cliffs and superb visibility, Puerto Rico offers great diving spots. The uninhabited Desecheo island is a perfect example, with 24 dive spots offering incredibly rich pickings.

- title: Toro Verde Zipline class: toro-verde-zipline content: >

Fly high above the ground on the longest zipline in the world. Stretching an adrenaline fueled 28 football pitches in length, this is one experience not to miss. The park itself offers a range of ziplines to try, plus other activities perfect for the thrill seekers amongst you.
